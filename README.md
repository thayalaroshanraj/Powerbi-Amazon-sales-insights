# 📊 Amazon Sales Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a Power BI dashboard built to analyze Amazon store sales data and extract meaningful business insights.
It focuses on understanding revenue trends, product performance, and regional sales behavior.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing categories and products
* Track monthly and seasonal trends
* Understand regional distribution of sales

---

## 📂 Dataset Details

* **File Name:** Amazon Store Sales Data.xlsx
* **File Size:** 768 KB
* **Number of Rows:** 5,902
* **Number of Columns:** 21
* **Format:** Excel (.xlsx)

### 📑 Key Fields

Sales, Profit, Quantity, Category, Sub-Category, Region, Order Date

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* **Power BI Desktop** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* **Microsoft Excel** – Data source

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

* Sales and profit overview
* Monthly sales trend analysis
* Category and sub-category performance
* Profit vs Sales comparison
* Regional sales distribution

---

## 🚀 Key Insights

* Sales show variation across months with higher performance in later periods
* Certain product categories contribute significantly more to total revenue
* Profit does not always scale with sales, indicating cost differences
* Regional distribution impacts overall sales performance
